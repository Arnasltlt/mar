About me

My name is Marius Valaitis and I am a Lithuanian furniture designer who also produces my own work. I founded Mar-Furniture to be able to share my work with the world. Most of the furniture I make is either entirely or partly recycled from the old furniture or scrap materials that I find available locally. This choice influences the design and reproducibility of my work, making some of it one of a kind. I see the history of the material as an added value to any furniture, an imprinted individualism. I believe that every piece of furniture created gets a piece of its creator, therefore I want to take part in each step of its creation. From sketching to the finishing touches.
